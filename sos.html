Who is Chris Hart?

Hi my name is Chris Hart.

Born and raised in Compton, CA by two hardworking parents. Father was a postal worker and mom a Day care owner. I have a beautiful wife Vicena and two kids, Nahla and CJ. I'm passionate about helping others reach their maximum potential. Prior to becoming an entreprenuer I worked 7 years in retail sales and 3 years in outside sales. At the age of 27 I decided to monetize my God given gifts and abilities and joined the PHP Agency. It's by far been the third best choice of my life. First, dedicating my life to Christ, second Marrying Vicena Hart, and third fathering 2 beautiful children.

"I decided to monetize my God given gifts and abilities".

What do I do?

I own an operate a Financial Marketing Orginazation. I teach families how to save money, make money, cover liabilites, and get out of debt. In a day and time where we don't learn about money in high school, college, or from our parents there's a huge need for financial literacy. My wife and I have decorated ourselves to serving others through financial education. Check out these videos on the importance of what i do.

What's my purpose?

My purpose is to serve.

My definate chief aim in life is to serve others. Our Orginazation SOS Financial Agency's sole purpose is to serve our Agents by giving them an opportunity to become true business owners and walk in their God given purpose, by utilizing their gifts to provide a lifestyle for their families.

How do we serve our clients?

We serve our clients by educating them on the key concepts that are stopping them from moving ahead in life. Like, Losing Money, Rising Healthcare Costs, Taxation, Inflation, and Procrastination with Finances. We serve our Carriers by introducing them to clients that need their services but are underserved due to their demographic. Millennials, Gen Xers, Founders, Minorities, and women are being left behind when it comes to financial literacy. Vicena and I lead these categories to serve the masses.
